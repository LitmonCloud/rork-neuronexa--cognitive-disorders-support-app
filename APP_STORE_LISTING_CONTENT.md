## App Store Optimization Tips

### Keyword Strategy

**Primary Keywords** (highest search volume):
- ADHD (extremely high)
- autism (extremely high)
- dementia (high)
- cognitive (medium)
- caregiver (medium)

**Secondary Keywords** (medium search volume):
- task manager
- mental health
- memory aid
- anxiety
- wellness

**Long-tail Keywords** (lower volume, higher conversion):
- executive function
- neurodivergent
- cognitive support
- alzheimer's care
- special needs

### Category Selection

**Primary Category:** Health & Fitness
**Secondary Category:** Medical

**Why these categories:**
- Medical: Establishes credibility for cognitive health
- Health & Fitness: Broader audience, includes wellness features

### Age Rating

**Expected:** 4+ or 12+

**Factors:**
- Medical/Treatment Information: Yes
- Unrestricted Web Access: No
- Made for Kids: No
